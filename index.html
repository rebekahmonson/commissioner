A collaborative mission for Miami arts

Commissioner will help you dive deep into our arts scene, build meaningful relationships with local artists and arts organizations, and begin your journey as an art collector. Together we will create, grow, and activate a new, engaged community of Miami patrons who are directly supporting Miami artists.

How it works

For an affordable quarterly subscription fee, commissioners will contribute to the commission of new work from a local artist and gain access to exclusive content and events with Miami galleries, artists, and arts institutions.

What artists are involved?

Commissioner's gallery partner is Primary Projects, which is currently recruiting artists for participation.

Who's behind this project?

Dejha Carrington is the executive director of Commmissioner. Commissioner is a joint project of Carrington Projects and WhereBy.Us.

Launching in early 2018

We're currently recruiting artists, event partners, and sponsors. If you're interested in working with us to make Commissioner a reality, email us.
